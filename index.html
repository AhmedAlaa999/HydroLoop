01 — About the project

Water in motion,
energy in return

HydroLoop Basin is a closed-loop hydroelectric demonstration model. Water is pumped to an elevated reservoir, then released to flow down and spin an overshot water wheel turbine — converting kinetic energy into measurable electrical output.

The system continuously recirculates water, making it a self-contained educational model for renewable energy principles, fluid mechanics, and electromagnetism.

03 — Operation

How it works

01

Pump Water Up

A small electric pump lifts water to the elevated reservoir, storing potential energy.

02

Flow Over Wheel

Water flows from the top, filling the overshot buckets and rotating the turbine wheel via gravity.

03

Generate Power

The spinning axle drives a generator, converting mechanical rotation into electrical energy output.
